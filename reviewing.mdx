# Review Board Procedures

<Info>
  This guide outlines the mandatory steps for organizing and conducting a Review Board. Adherence to these procedures is critical to maintaining fairness and operational integrity.
</Info>

## 1. Organizing & Scheduling

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Setup">
    Coordinate a time that accommodates the user under review, yourself, and a designated Leadership member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Ticket">
    Create a `Management Ticket` and invite the user under review to begin the coordination process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finalize Time">
    Once the schedule is confirmed, proceed with forming your Review Board based on the agreed-upon time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 2. Review Board Composition

<Warning>
  All members selected for a Review Board must have **no relation** to the case or the individual under review (e.g., no personal friendships). Furthermore, active Staff members from Internal Affairs are ineligible, and no participant can have a history with the original case.
</Warning>

To conduct a board, you are required to have **5 participants** confirmed and vetted by the supervising Leadership member:

| Role | Required Count |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Leadership** | 1 |
| **Management** | 1 |
| **Active Staff** | 1 |
| **Former/Active Member** | 2 |

- **Vetting Process:** Once participants are selected, add them to the ticket and ask the user under review if they have objections.
- **Objections:** The user may request alternative members, but they are **not** permitted to request new Leadership or Management representatives.

## 3. Conducting the Review

<Card title="Preparation Checklist" icon="list-check">
  Ensure the following items are prepared **10 minutes** before the scheduled start time:

  - [**Review Board Guide**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/19v-iIGlave04KIXz4V3mvxElDjLt1C3JlOb9dsJrJMM/edit?usp=sharing)
  - [**Review Board Form**](https://forms.gle/HnJ2MrAKMiLg2kkR9)
  - **Evidence Access:** Direct view of the user's specific Review Board infraction.
</Card>

## 4. Post-Review Procedures (Added)

Once the Review Board concludes:

1. **Submit Findings:** Ensure the Review Board Form is submitted immediately following the discussion.
2. **Close Ticket:** Only close the ticket once the Leadership member confirms the decision has been logged and the user has been notified.
3. **Internal Log:** Update the management log with the final verdict and participant list for accountability.

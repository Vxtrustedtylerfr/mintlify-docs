# Tasks & Responsibilities

<Info>
  Below is a comprehensive list of tasks and duties associated with each role. Additional tasks may be assigned as needed. All listed tasks are mandatory for their respective roles.
</Info>

## Weekly Operations

<Card title="Weekly Tasks">
  Management tasks are assigned on a weekly basis, ranging from Staff Supervision to Discord Management. These are overseen and assigned by Directors or the Senior Management team to ensure operational consistency.
</Card>

## Departmental Duties

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Community Management">
    The Management Team handles all Discord-related operations, including staff supervision and ticket resolution.

    - **Discord Issues:** Handle all server-related concerns.
    - **Staff Supervision:** Manage and support Discord staff.
    - **Tickets:** Manage and resolve management-level tickets.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Staff Supervision">
    Staff Supervision oversees all operational aspects regarding staff conduct and performance.

    - **Collaboration:** Work closely with the Management Team on operational changes.
    - **Accountability:** Issue weekly quota infractions.
    - **Tickets:** Handle management-level tickets.
    - **Oversight:** Ensure all staff adhere to Rules and Regulations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

  <Expandable title="Escalation Path">
    <Card title="🚨Escalation Path">
      <Expandable title="When you encounter an issue that exceeds your current authority:" defaultOpen>
        1. **Level 1 (Routine):** Document the issue in the team management log.
        2. **Level 2 (Urgent):** Contact your direct Staff Supervisor for immediate guidance.
        3. **Level 3 (Critical):** If an issue poses a threat to server safety, notify a Director immediately via the private emergency channel.
      </Expandable>
    </Card>
  </Expandable>
</Card>

<Card title="Daily Procedures">
  <Expandable title="Daily Procedures">
    <Card title="🛠️ Daily Procedures">
      <Expandable title="Procedures" defaultOpen>
        To maintain server vitality, all High Ranking members are expected to perform the following daily:

        - **Audit:** Conduct at least one sweep of active channels to ensure compliance with server rules.
        - **Support:** Check the ticket queue for pending requests that require management attention.
        - **Engagement:** Maintain an active presence in public channels to facilitate community interaction.
      </Expandable>
    </Card>
  </Expandable>

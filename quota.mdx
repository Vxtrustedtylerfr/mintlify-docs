<Info>
  Each rank is assigned specific quotas. Please familiarize yourself with the requirements for your role. If you are uncertain about your obligations, please reach out to a senior team member for guidance.
</Info>

## Role Requirements

<Steps>
  <Step title="Public Relations">
    **Weekly Requirements:**

    - **Time:** 2 hours/week.
    - **Support:** Handle a minimum of 3 support tickets/week while maintaining professional, timely, and high-quality responses.
    - **Engagement:** Send 50 messages/week in public channels while actively assisting and engaging with the community.
    - **Tasks:** Must complete all assignments, announcements, and projects assigned by PR Leadership or the High Rank Team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Management">
    **Weekly Requirements:**

    - **Time:** 1 hour/week.
    - **Engagement:** 70 messages/week in public channels.
    - **Tasks:** Must fulfill all tasks assigned by the Executive Team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Oversight & Vitality Roles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Operations" icon="gear">
    - **Quota:** No set numeric quota.
    - **Focus:** Maintain server activity and vitality.
    - **Oversight:** Ensure the Management Team upholds server values and rules.

  <Card title="Executives" icon="user-tie">

## Performance Tracking

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Performance Tracking" defaultOpen>
    - **Deadline:** All quotas are reviewed weekly every Sunday at 11:59 PM EST.
    - **Logging:** Ensure all activity is documented in the designated management logs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title=" Policy on Missed Quotas" defaultOpen>
    Failure to meet quotas results in the following:

    - **First Offense:** Verbal warning and extension.
    - **Second Offense:** Formal write-up and access restriction.
    - **Third Offense:** Permanent removal from the High Ranking team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

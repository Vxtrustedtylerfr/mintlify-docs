# Team Policies & Conduct

<Warning>
  These policies are mandatory for all Networkerlc High Ranking team members. Failure to adhere to these standards may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
</Warning>

## Ticket Protocols

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Management Guidelines">
    - **Access:** All Management members have access to General Support and Management tickets.
    - **Claiming:** Staff must claim tickets to prevent overlap; unclaimed tickets are open to all.
    - **Interrupting:** Do not interrupt an ongoing ticket unless you hold a Directive role.
    - **SPaG:** Proper spelling, punctuation, and grammar are mandatory in all interactions.
    - **Authority:** Only designated Staff Supervisors (SS) are authorized to handle MGMT-level tickets.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Public Relations">
    - **Ticket Access:** PR members may access support tickets, but only PR Leadership can manage administrative or staff-related tickets.
    - **Interference:** Any attempt to influence, interfere with, or access unauthorized tickets will result in immediate administrative leave pending investigation.
    - **Discipline:** Unauthorized interference will result in a formal infraction and potential termination.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## General Conduct Expectations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Professionalism & Ethics" icon="user-tie">
    High Ranking members are expected to act as role models. This includes maintaining a neutral stance in disputes, avoiding unprofessional language, and upholding the integrity of the Networkerlc community at all times.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Confidentiality" icon="lock">
    All discussions regarding administrative decisions, staff infractions, or internal management logistics are strictly confidential. Do not leak internal data to staff or the general public.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Chain of Command" icon="sitemap">
    Always respect the chain of command. If you have an issue with a peer, attempt to resolve it privately before escalating to a Director or Senior Manager.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
